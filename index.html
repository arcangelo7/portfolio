About Me

Digital Humanities Researcher

For most of my time, I am a researcher in Digital Humanities. I specialize in leveraging Semantic Web technologies to enhance bibliographic metadata. I work primarily with OpenCitations, an infrastructure that publishes open bibliographic citation metadata. Our mission is to revolutionize academic publishing by making data freely accessible, challenging the paywalls of for-profit organizations that hinder academic research.

Founder of AstroGod

In my free time, I founded AstroGod, a platform offering personalized and advanced interpretations of natal charts, synastries, couple charts, and future predictions. Together with my collaborator, Jacopo Trombetti, we have established a rigorous method of astrological interpretation, utilizing AI to generate coherent and user-friendly texts.

Supporting Maestra Cavallerizza

I also support Maestra Cavallerizza, a project aimed at improving parent-child relationships through interactions with horses. The horse acts as a mirror to the child, helping to develop empathy.

Electronic Music Producer

Recently, I started producing electronic music to convey my favorite texts from Latin and Italian literature. I’ve just released my first single.
